What is the difference between var and let?
Var allows for reassignment and redeclaration. But you cannot redeclare with let. 

What is the difference between var and const?
Var allows for reassignment and redeclaration. But const cannot reassign and redeclare const. 

What is the difference between let and const?
You can reassign with let, but you can not redeclare or reassign using the const keyword.

What is hoisting?
Variables are “hoisted” to the top of the scope they are declared in. Also you can access the variable value before it is used later on.
